Tumble Sort

A delightful, physics-based puzzle experience.

The Gameplay

Tumble Sort is designed to be instantly playable but challenging to master. Guide falling items—from dogs and cats to cows—into their correct bins using intuitive physics mechanics. Perfect for short bursts of gameplay or long, relaxing puzzle sessions.

Features

  • Intuitive Physics: Natural, satisfying item drops and bounces.
  • Progressive Difficulty: The speed and complexity scale as you play.
  • Premium Polish: Zero intrusive ads, just clean, uninterrupted gameplay.
