Tumble Sort

Tumble. Bounce. Sort. An engaging tactile puzzle for early learners.

Cognitive Growth

Learn Through Play

With up to 16 distinct categories, kids build visual associations, expand their vocabulary, and practice rapid categorization. A built-in distractor system encourages higher-order thinking beyond simple matching.

Inclusive Design

Pre-Reader Friendly

An Emoji Mode toggle replaces text labels with visual icons, allowing toddlers to play independently. Tactile haptic feedback reinforces successful drops, grounding digital physics with real-world responses.

Sensory Balance

Calming Audio

Designed to be engaging without causing sensory overload. Tumble Sort features a warm acoustic soundtrack and gentle, rewarding sound effects that keep toddlers focused without driving parents crazy.
