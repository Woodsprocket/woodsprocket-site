Shade Shape

Rotate. Align. Reveal. A 3D spatial puzzle of light and shadow.

Cognitive Growth

Spatial Reasoning

Players manipulate 3D objects to perfectly align with complex silhouettes. This hands-on geometry develops critical visual-spatial awareness and advanced problem-solving skills in a highly engaging, educational environment.

Play Your Way

Race or Relax

Challenge yourself to beat the clock and earn speed stars across a finite campaign of carefully crafted levels. Prefer a calmer experience? Simply turn off the timer for a completely stress-free, untimed puzzle session.

Customization

Premium Glow Ups

Make the game entirely your own by purchasing optional "Glow Ups." These custom cosmetic skins let you personalize your 3D shapes with vibrant new materials, adding an extra layer of visual fun to your puzzle-solving.
