Tumble Sort

Tumble Sort is a physics-based puzzle game designed exclusively for iOS. The game drops a random item from a selected category, and the player must tilt their device to steer the item into the correct bucket before it falls off the screen.

Blaster Blitz

Release Date: June 2026
Platform: iOS
App Store: View Listing

Blaster Blitz is a premium, action-packed arcade game designed exclusively for iOS. Players use intuitive tilt controls to steer their rocket and tap to shoot obstacles. In Math Mode, an equation appears at the top of the screen, and the player must rapidly calculate the answer and steer their ship through the correct falling gate.
