Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

Woodsprocket Studios LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates woodsprocket.com and develops mobile applications. We are committed to protecting your privacy through data minimization—meaning we only collect the absolute minimum data necessary to provide our Services.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

Woodsprocket Studios is built on a privacy-first philosophy. We do not collect names, mailing addresses, or phone numbers. We do not use third-party trackers or "fingerprinting" to identify you or your device across other apps or websites. Our current games do not require user accounts.

2. Information From Third Parties

Apple App Store: If you purchase a game or in-app item, Apple processes that transaction. We receive anonymized sales reports and performance data, but we never see your credit card or billing details.

Infrastructure: Our domain and hosting providers (Namecheap/GitHub) may collect standard log files (IP addresses, browser type) for security and performance monitoring.

3. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are intended for a general audience. In compliance with COPPA and global safety standards, we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Our apps use Apple’s native age-rating systems to ensure content appropriateness.

4. Your Rights

Regardless of your location, you have the right to know what data is being collected. Because we do not collect personal identifiers, your "Right to be Forgotten" is the default state of our studio.
